PROBLEM STATEMENT:

You have to take 1 integer from user input. That will be the radius
of a circle. You have to print out the area of circle based on
the taken radius.

HINT:
formula for area of circle is πr².
here in this formula, r is the radius.

Sample Input 1:
34
Sample Output 1:
3629.84

Sample Input 2:
2
Sample Output 2:
12.56

NOTE: you can not use any builtIn libraries or modules
for this problem.
